All Pierogi Kitchen: A Different Taste of Europe

#ukrainian #polish #lunchspecials

Introduction

Cuisine: Ukrainian/Polish (European)
Address: 1245 W Baseline Rd STE #101-103, Mesa, AZ 85202
Website: All Pierogi Kitchen

  Drive a few roads down to Mesa, and find yourself in an authentic European eatery! A rich, hearty cuisine that offers flavors completely unique from the Italian or French dishes you may be used to, All Pierogi Kitchen is a culinary adventure off the beaten path. Get ready to dig into meat, potatoes, bread-- and, of course, pierogi!-- and discover another country to add to your dream Europe trip!

My Recommendations

  You've probably already guessed from the restaurant's name, but their specialty is their handmade pierogi! With 15 combinations to choose from composed of sauerkraut, vegetables, cheese, and meat, you're sure to find the pierogi of your dreams. There are even dessert pierogi available such as apple cinnamon, sweet cheese, cherry, plum, and blueberry. (And, a quick tip from me: always get the pierogi fried when you're given the choice between boiled and fried. While they are still delicious boiled, frying the pierogi in butter gives them a delicious crust and texture.)
Considering the pierogi are already the star of the show, I'm sure I don't need to highlight my favorites. Instead, the list below are some of my go-to dishes when I'm feeling like something else (I still get my share of pierogi by stealing from my siblings' plates!):

  • Beef Goulash (a slow cooked beef stew)
  • Combo Plate (comes with a Cabbage Roll and 6 Pierogi)
  • Plov (a spiced rice dish with beef)

A Tip for You!

Can't have enough delicious pierogi? Check out the adjoining Euro Market right next to the entrance! You can buy an assortment of sausages, fresh breads, refrigerated dishes, and frozen bags of hand-made pierogi and pelmeni that are just as delicious as in the restaurant. Polish and Ukrainian desserts, pickles, teas, and pastries can be found along the shelves as well. To find an absolute gem, though, look to the refrigerated section near the back right corner-- they also sell homemade sheets of Honey Cake, a wonderfully fluffy but not-too-sweet cake! It's sold by weight, and usually comes out to around $25. Indulge in it with a cup of coffee or tea!
