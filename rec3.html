MochiFresh: All-Natural Homemade Flavored Boba

#asian #boba #fruity

Introduction

Cuisine: Asian (Taiwanese/Vietnamese)
Address: 960 W University Dr #114, Tempe, AZ 85281
Website: MochiFresh

  Feeling like a sweet treat to top off a successful study session? My absolute favorite boba spot, of all the shops I've tried within the Phoenix area and across the world, is located just a couple of streets away from ASU! Offering a wide selection of fruit teas, milk teas, smoothies, and homemade toppings, MochiFresh stands apart with its flavorful drinks that always feel refreshing. If you're also looking for a bite to eat, they have that covered as well! Mochi ice cream, coconut jelly (in an adorable cat shape!), and various hot side dishes like gyoza or takoyaki are also available. There's also ample seating, so it's a great place to go with friends! Check out the video on the left by @saturday_faturday on Instagram for an overview of MochiFresh!

My Recommendations

  Like most boba places, you can customize the sugar levels, ice levels, and toppings for your drink. I'm usually the type to drop sugar to 50% or 25%, but at MochiFresh, I never need to adjust! Every drink is carefully balanced to be wholesome, sweet, and refreshing, all without being cloyingly sweet. I'd suggest trying out the drink as is before jumping to your preferred sugar or ice levels.
And just like their drink menu, when it comes to toppings, MochiFresh absolutely excels! The boba is always soft and chewy, so you never have to worry about hard, old boba. The custard pudding may sound like an odd choice to put in a drink if you're not familiar with it, but it's actually a wonderfully silky addition!
Thinking of all the options can get overwhelming, so I'm glad that MochiFresh already puts great arrangements together, with drinks usually coming with default toppinsg (no extra toppings charge!). These are some of my absolute favorites:

  • Banana Bubble Milk (comes with pudding and brown sugar boba)
  • Mango Bubble Milk (comes with mango boba)
  • Real Taro Milk Shake + added strawberry boba

  The Banana Bubble Milk is especially wonderful, with a slightly frothy/creamy texture from the fresh bananas added to it. No matter the drink, though, I usually end up finishing it off in a couple of minutes and wishing I'd bought another!

A Tip for You!

If you become a MochiFresh addict (as I have), then keep an eye out near the end of the year! MochiFresh has a gift card sale where you can get a bonus amount on the gift card. Plus, since the gift card never expires, there's no rush to use up the whole amount. The amounts are as follows:

  • $50 for a $55 gift card (free $5)
  • $100 for a $120 gift card (free $20)
  • $200 for a $300 gift card (free $100!)

And if you're lucky enough to stop by on a day when there's fresh mochi, definitely try some! They're soft, squishy, and have a gentle sweetness. Flavors include red bean, black sesame, and taro, but it depends on what they've made that day!
